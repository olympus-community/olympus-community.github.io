Welcome

Official wiki for the framework and Half-Life Roleplay server. This wiki serves as an informative resource for both the developers and players, featuring guides, tips, tricks, and more. Created by the olympus owner and developer, UniQodex.

impulse-inspired ✅

olympus, although completely different framework, is very similar in design to the Impulse UI—but more modern and visually fitting.

Revamped HLRP 🚀

Taking the best from semi-serious and serious roleplay, we removed boring and tedious features and fused everything together—creating a fresh way to experience the Half-Life roleplay genre.

Fast & Lightweight ⚡

The framework easily handles 128 players while being incredibly fast during in-game events. With a focus on server-side and client-side performance, even players with low-end laptops can enjoy olympus. It features graphical settings that even GMod normal settings doesn't offer, along with support for older hardware with integrated graphical card.
